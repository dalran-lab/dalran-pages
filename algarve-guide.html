🇵🇹 Algarve Family Guide

Curated by Sébastien & Tania · Prioritised for kids aged 2 & 4
📍 Base camp · 3HB Guaraná, Olhos de Água, Albufeira
⭐ Must Do
1
Alvor Beach & Boardwalk
Alvor · Portimão
🚗 35 km · 35 min 👶 Toddler-perfect 💛 Sébastien's top pick

Endless sandy beach with a km-long wooden boardwalk ideal for little ones to run and explore. Go late afternoon: walk the boardwalk at sunset, then dinner in the lively village. Sébastien is here right now with Tania and highly recommends it.

🍽️
À Ribeira do Alvor — Sébastien's restaurant pick. Order the ceviche, cataplana (the local specialty) and the famous white sangria. Village is lively after for ice cream.
📍 Map – À Ribeira do Alvor
📍 Alvor Beach on Google Maps
2
Carvoeiro & Clifftop Walk
Carvoeiro · Lagoa — 20 min from hotel
🚗 17 km · 20 min 👶 Great with young kids

The closest highlight to your hotel. Carvoeiro is one of the most picturesque coastal villages in the Algarve, with a dramatic clifftop wooden walkway (passadico) hugging the golden cliffs over the sea. Easy half-day, worth a dinner.

🚶
Clifftop wooden walkway — The main attraction. Walk over the cliffs to Praia de Carvoeiro below. Kids love it.
📍 Carvoeiro on Google Maps
3
Ferragudo
Ferragudo · Lagoa
🚗 22 km · 25 min 👶 Charming harbour village

A beautiful, unspoiled fishing village with whitewashed houses and a pretty historic waterfront. Kids love watching the fishing boats. Far less touristy than Albufeira — perfect for a relaxed lunch stop.

🍝
Borda do Cais — Sébastien's restaurant pick. Famous for spaghetti à la cataplana. Right on the waterfront.
📍 Map – Borda do Cais
📍 Ferragudo on Google Maps
4
Lagos & Ponta de Piedade
Lagos · Western Algarve
🚗 48 km · 45 min ⛵ Boat trip with toddlers! 💛 Sébastien's Algarve #1

Sébastien's favourite in the whole Algarve. Beautiful village centre with restaurants and shops, and the famous Ponta de Piedade sea caves just outside. For the sea caves, negotiate a boat trip directly with local fishermen at the river — big hit with kids. Or drive to Ponta de Piedade and walk the wooden clifftop pontons for the same dramatic views without the boat.

⛵
Ponta de Piedade sea caves — Negotiate directly with fishermen at the Lagos riverfront harbour. Boat trip into the caves is the most fun option for families.
📍 Map – Ponta de Piedade
📍 Lagos on Google Maps
🚗 Worth the Trip
5
Praia da Rocha & Portimão Beaches
Portimão area
🚗 30 km · 30 min 👶 Big sandy beaches

The long stretch of beaches between Lagos and Praia da Rocha is among the most beautiful in the Algarve — wide golden sand and sheltered bays, great for building sandcastles. Sébastien doesn't recommend Portimão city itself but the beaches are excellent.

🐟
Forte e Feio (Portimão) — Sébastien's pick for ultra-fresh fish. Worth a lunch stop.
📍 Map – Forte e Feio
📍 Praia da Rocha on Google Maps
6
Olhão & Ria Formosa
Olhão · Eastern Algarve
🚗 52 km · 45 min ⛵ Boat trip in nature park

From Olhão, take a boat into the Ria Formosa natural marine reserve — a stunning lagoon system with barrier islands, sandbars and wildlife. A memorable half-day excursion. Combine with Tavira for a full eastern Algarve day.

📍 Olhão on Google Maps
7
Tavira
Tavira · Eastern Algarve
🚗 75 km · 55 min 👣 Lovely stroll, calm pace

One of the most charming historic towns in the Algarve — Roman bridge, white-cube Moorish architecture and a peaceful river. Pair with Olhão on the same day for a full eastern Algarve trip.

📍 Tavira on Google Maps
8
Algarve Shopping
Guia · 10 min from hotel
🚗 8 km · 10 min 🌧️ Rainy day lifesaver

The closest shopping mall — just 10 minutes from 3HB Guaraná. Your go-to if the weather turns or you need anything. Air-conditioned with restaurants and a kids' play area.

📍 Algarve Shopping on Google Maps
🌥️ Optional / Weather dependent
9
Forum Algarve
Faro
🚗 40 km · 35 min 🌧️ Bigger rainy day option

The best shopping mall in the Algarve near Faro. Bigger and better than Guia with a wide choice of shops and restaurants. Good for a rainy afternoon or an evening out.

📍 Forum Algarve on Google Maps
10
Sagres
Southwestern tip of Europe
🚗 92 km · 1h 15 min ⚠️ Long drive, less for toddlers

Dramatic clifftop fortress at the very southwestern tip of Europe — genuinely spectacular raw scenery. A long drive and limited activities for 2–4 year olds. Better as a couple's excursion, or keep for a long clear day when you feel adventurous.

📍 Sagres on Google Maps
11
Odeceixe & Aljezur
Wild West Coast
🚗 95–100 km · 1h 20 min ⚠️ Sébastien: probably too far with kids

Wild, authentic and beautifully preserved west coast — a world away from tourist Algarve. Odeceixe is a surf hotspot. Sébastien himself flags it as probably too far with young kids and no real activities for them. Keep as a spontaneous adventure if the mood strikes.
